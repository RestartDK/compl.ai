## TIER 1: INVESTMENT BANKING (Most Restrictive)

### Rule 1.1: Active Deal Prohibition
**Status:** ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION

Employees on active deals CANNOT trade any securities related to that deal.

**Current Active Deals:**
- DEAL001: CloudVision AI (CVAI) - IPO - Expected Close: 2025-02-15
- DEAL002: DataStream Technologies (DSTM) - M&A Sell Side - Expected Close: 2025-01-30
- DEAL003: BioNova Therapeutics - Private Placement - Expected Close: 2025-01-15
- Multiple ECM deals (see deal pipeline)

**Consequences:** Violation results in immediate suspension of trading privileges and potential termination.

---

### Rule 1.2: Coverage Universe Prohibition
**Status:** ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION

Tier 1 IB employees CANNOT trade any securities in their assigned coverage universe.

**Coverage Universes by Department:**
- Technology IB: All enterprise software, cloud, AI/ML companies (16+ restricted stocks)
- Healthcare IB: All pharmaceutical, biotech, medical device, healthcare services companies (15+ restricted stocks)
- ECM: All companies in active banking pipeline (100+ securities)

**Examples:**
- Technology banker cannot trade: AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, NVDA, META, AMZN, TSLA, ORCL, CRM, ADBE, INTC, AMD, SNOW, PLTR
- Healthcare banker cannot trade: PFE, JNJ, UNH, LLY, ABBV, MRK, TMO, ABT, AMGN, GILD, BMY, CVS, REGN, VRTX

**Consequences:** Violation is a FINRA violation and grounds for immediate termination.

---

### Rule 1.3: Recent Pitch Restriction
**Status:** TIME-BASED RESTRICTION

Employees cannot trade securities from pitches they made within 60 days of the pitch date.

**Current Restricted Pitches:**
- MDTK (MedTech Innovations): Pitched 2024-10-20, Restriction ends 2024-12-20 (EXPIRED)

**Rationale:** Prevents trading on information disclosed to clients during pitches.

---

### Rule 1.4: Broad Market Index Funds (Limited Permission)
**Status:** PERMITTED WITH CONDITIONS

Tier 1 IB can trade broad market index funds (SPY, VOO, VTI, etc.) with pre-clearance.

**Conditions:**
- Requires 24-hour pre-clearance
- 90-day minimum holding period
- Maximum position: $500,000 daily value
- Approval valid for 5 business days only

**Approved List:** SPY, VOO, VTI, IVV, SCHX

---

### Rule 1.5: Individual Stock Trading
**Status:** PROHIBITED

No individual stock trading except broad index funds and sector allocation per Rule 1.4.

## TIER 2: RESEARCH, TRADING, PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT (High Restrictions)

### Rule 2.1: Research Analyst Coverage Prohibition (FINRA 2241)
**Status:** ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION

Research analysts CANNOT trade any security they cover.

**Coverage Restrictions:**
- Jennifer Martinez (Tech Analyst): AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, META, NVDA (5 securities)
- Alicia Ramirez (Financial Institutions Analyst): JPM, BAC, WFC, C, GS, MS, BLK, SCHW, USB, PNC, TFC, COF, AXP, BK, STT (15 securities)

**Quiet Period:** 90 days after dropping coverage of a security.

**Example:** Jennifer Martinez cannot trade any of her 5 covered stocks under ANY circumstances.

---

### Rule 2.2: Research Analyst Sector Restriction
**Status:** RESTRICTION

Research analysts CANNOT trade ANY stock in their coverage sector (beyond their specific coverage list).

**Example:** Tech analyst cannot trade ANY tech stock (not just the 5 covered).

---

### Rule 2.3: Quiet Period Restriction
**Status:** TIME-BASED RESTRICTION

During earnings preview quiet periods, analysts cannot trade covered securities.

**Current Quiet Periods:**
- NVDA: Quiet period 2025-01-16 to 2025-03-02 (Jennifer Martinez)

---

### Rule 2.4: Trader Product Prohibition
**Status:** ABSOLUTE PROHIBITION

Traders CANNOT trade securities from their assigned trading desk.

**Example:** David Kumar (Technology Desk Trader) cannot trade 22 tech stocks he actively trades.

**Permission:** Can trade different asset classes (bonds, commodities, managed accounts) with pre-clearance.

---

### Rule 2.5: Portfolio Manager Client Portfolio Prohibition
**Status:** RESTRICTION

Portfolio Managers CANNOT trade securities held in client portfolios they manage.

**Client Portfolio Example:** Amanda Foster manages Large Cap Growth fund with positions in AAPL (8.2%), MSFT, NVDA, GOOGL, AMZN, META, TSLA, V, MA, JPM, UNH, LLY, COST, HD, PG.

**Restriction:** Cannot trade ANY of these 15 securities while managing the portfolio.

**7-Day Lag Rule:** Must wait 7 days after client trades before trading the same security in personal account.

---

### Rule 2.6: Tier 2 Pre-Clearance Requirement
**Status:** REQUIRED FOR ALL TRADES

All Tier 2 employees require pre-clearance for individual stock trades.

**Process:**
1. Submit pre-clearance request via portal
2. Provide business justification
3. Allow 24-48 hours for review
4. Approval valid for 5 business days

## PRE-CLEARANCE PROCESS

### Rule 9.1: When Pre-Clearance is Required
- ✅ Tier 1: All trades except prohibited
- ✅ Tier 2: All individual stock trades
- ✅ Tier 3: Individual stock trades
- ✅ Tier 4: Trades >$50,000 or financial sector

### Rule 9.2: Pre-Clearance Timeline
- Standard: 24-48 hours
- Complex: 48-72 hours
- High Confidence Approvals: Same day possible

### Rule 9.3: Approval Validity
- Pre-clearance valid for 5 business days
- Must execute within window
- Reapply if window expires

## DAILY TRADING LIMITS

### Rule 10.1: Position Limits
- Maximum daily trade value: $500,000
- Maximum position in single security: 10% of fund AUM
- Breach triggers automatic compliance review

## REPORTING REQUIREMENTS

### Rule 11.1: Trade Reporting Threshold
- Trades exceeding $10,000 require disclosure
- Submit within 10 days of trade
- Used for monitoring and audit trail

### Rule 11.2: Monthly Compliance Report
- All pre-clearances granted/denied
- All blackout period compliance
- All MNPI disclosures
- Generated by Compliance department

## PENALTIES FOR VIOLATIONS

### Rule 12.1: Enforcement
- **Minor Violation (First):** Written warning + trading suspension 30 days
- **Serious Violation:** Trading suspension 90 days + financial penalty
- **Egregious Violation:** Termination + regulatory referral to SEC/FINRA

### Rule 12.2: Audit Trail
- All trading activity monitored
- All pre-clearances logged
- All violations documented
- Subject to regulatory examination
